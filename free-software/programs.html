Here's a list of programs that I use and recommend.

  • Window Manager: xmonad with xmobar
  • IDE: emacs, vim
  • Editor: nano
  • Terminal: xterm
  • Shell: bash
  • Music Player: mpd + mpc
  • Video Player: vlc
  • Browser: Firefox with privacy addons.
  • Email: mutt, mbsync, msmtp with a service provider like posteo.de that respects your privacy.
  • Internet Search: duckduckgo
  • Encryption: GnuPG
  • Password Manager: pass
